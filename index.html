July 1, 2025

Pinballers Releases

Pinballers, the incremental roguelike developed and published
by DragonkidGames, launches on Steam today for Windows, Mac and
Linux. The game will be released with 4 machines and 8 pinballs to unlock,
along with 1 starting machine and 1 pinball.

June 2, 2025

Pinballers Demo Available Now

The demo for Pinballers is now available, and can be downloaded here. The demo features the 1st machine, including all component unlocks and classic mode for that machine.
