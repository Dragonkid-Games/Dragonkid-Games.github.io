Madness Machine Announced for Pinballers

The next machine has been announced for Pinballers. This will be the Madness machine, and will feature a new component, the multiplier. This is also the first machine to have a permanent component unlocked from the beginning of the game. The update is scheduled for July 31, and will also feature new pinball designs, a couple of codes and some quality of life fixes.
